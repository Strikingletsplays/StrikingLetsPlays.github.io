Expertise

I focus on creating Games that are fun, unique & enjoyable to play!

Designing and developing video games, is an amazing experience.

  • Planning a video game

    Ideas! Before begining the development of a game, a game idea is needed. Let the game design minds run free! 2D? 3D! Type? features! ... character? location.. target audience?.. All these questions need to be answerd!

  • Pre-production

    Found an Idea? Solidify it! Find the narrative of the story and backstories, relationships, art styles of the game, physics.. What is the "fun factor" of the game?

  • Production

    Okay.. Fun part begins! Creating & Iterating designs to bring your game ideas to life. All visuals, audio level designs, and initial coding is brought forward.

  • Testing

    Playtesting prototypes to find bugs, wonky mechanics and most importantly.. finding out if your game is Fun!
